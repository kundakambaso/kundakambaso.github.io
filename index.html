Hi! Welcome to my homepage.

I am a PhD student in the working group of Ghislain Fourier in Aachen, Germany. My research lies in the intersection of representation theory of Lie algebras, algebraic geometry and combinatorics. More specifically, I study PBW degenerations of Lie algebras and thier associated PBW degenerate varieties (flag varieties and Schubert varieties). The main methods in use come from combinatorics (polytopes, Young Tableaux,...) and use of mathematical software.
